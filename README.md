# FurSure – Smart Pet Health Collar

## Overview
FurSure is a conceptual smart pet health collar startup developed as part of a university entrepreneurship and innovation capstone. As CEO of a 5-person team, I led market analysis, pricing strategy, and sales planning for a smart IoT collar tracking activity, vitals, sleep, and GPS location.

## My Role

* Led market analysis, pricing strategy, and sales plan
* Presented full investor pitch to a mixed technical and business audience
